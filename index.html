The History

Founded in 2004, out of a passion for stripping away the extraneous, Plated is an exercise in restraint and sophistication. Focused on a mission to take simplicity to new heights, we create meals that are considered, precise, and enduring.

Plated continues to redefine modern European cuisine, one beautifully designed dish at a time.

The Signature Experience

Precision. Restraint. Purpose. Every plate at Plated is a deliberate union of flavor and texture, drawn from the traditions of Europe but brought into the present with a modern twist. No excess, only clean and concentrated artisanship.

The Questions

“ Do I need a reservation? ”

Reservations are recommended, but we always leave room for walk-ins. Secure your table for an uninterrupted dining experience.

“ Does Plated accommodate dietary restrictions? ”

Absolutely. Our menu includes vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options, and we’re happy to adjust dishes where possible. Let us know when you book or inform your server.

“ Is there a dress code? ”

Smart casual. We want you to feel comfortable while embracing the refined atmosphere of Plated.

“ Do you offer takeout? ”

Our dishes are designed to be experienced fresh, but select items are available for takeaway. Call ahead for availability.
